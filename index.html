Hatter & The Boys Go West!!!

This is one of several roadtrips taken by Rebecca, Rafe, & Kyle in an effort to visit every state in the continental United States as a trio.

  • Oklahoma
  • Texas
  • New Mexico
  • Arizona
  • Utah
  • Colorado
  • Kansas

Oklahoma

Birthplace of Roger Miller, King of the Road!

While in Oklahoma we visited the statehouse in Oklahoma City, saw a large blue whale and camped at Lake Thunderbird State Park. We also spent an afternoon in Erick, which is the birthplace of music legend Roger Miller.
Picture from Oklahoma!

Texas

Everything is bigger in Texas!

In Texas we saw a lot of cattle that were tightly herded together in large pens. We also kissed a piece of the Blarney Stone in Shamrock and saw a lot of the inspiration for Cars' Radiator Springs.
Pictures from Texas!

New Mexico

The Very Large Array!!!

We fell in love with New Mexico. It was very quiet. We camped under the stars after visiting the Very Large Array. We saw aliens at the UFO Museum & travelled so close to the Mexican border that we were questioned by Border Patrol.
Pictures from New Mexico!

Arizona

The Grand Canyon!!

In Arizona we visited Petrified Forest National Park as well as the Grand Canyon. We saw an abundance of natural wonders as well as ancient man-made dwellings and drawings.We even visited the corner from a song in Winslow and saw a massive meteor crater.
Pictures from Arizona!

Utah

Doctor Who filmed on location here in Utah!

In Utah we visited 3 Doctor Who filming locations and even camped on Lone Rock Beach! Monument Valley was gorgeous and we enjoyed it and Arches National Park even though Rebecca seriously injured her leg.
Pictures from Utah!

Colorado

Home of Casa Bonita!!

We made it to Casa Bonita, had sopapillas, took old-timey photos, saw a gunfight, a diver, and an escaped gorilla! We even drove by Rebecca's old high school.
Pictures from Colorado!

Kansas

Dorothy slept here!

In Kansas, we saw the statehouse in Topeka and a lovely rainbow painted house that sits across the street from a hate-filled church. We saw "Wheat Jesus" and some other religious signs.
Pictures from Kansas!
